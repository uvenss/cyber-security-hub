3. Rational Approach & Mindset

Cyber security isn't just about tools; it's about a careful, rational, and persistent mindset. Always seek official, verified sources for information.

  • **Continuous Learning:** The cyber world changes daily.
  • **Legality First:** Always operate within legal boundaries.
  • **Documentation:** Good hackers document their findings.
